# AgriSmartSA – AI-Powered Automated Watering System 🌿

**Huawei ICT Competition 2025 – 2026**  
**Cape Peninsula University of Technology | 石华智能 (Shí Huá SmartTech)**  

---

## 📌 Project Overview
AgriSmartSA is an AI-powered smart irrigation system designed to help farmers **grow more food using less water**. It addresses real-world challenges such as **water scarcity, unpredictable rainfall, and manual irrigation**, which often lead to **water waste, low yields, and high labor costs**.  

The system uses **sensors and AI** to monitor soil and environmental conditions in real-time, making **automatic watering decisions** based on crop and soil needs. By turning guesswork into **data-driven farming**, it helps farmers save water, reduce costs, and improve crop health.

---

## 🎯 Key Features
- Real-time monitoring of soil moisture, pH, temperature, and humidity  
- AI-powered decisions on watering time and duration  
- Automatic water pump activation based on sensor data  
- Tracks environmental data and stores it in the app for analysis  
- Improves crop yield, saves water (~40%), and reduces labor costs  
- Modular and scalable design for small plots to large farms  

---

## 🔧 Tech Stack
- **Microcontroller**: Arduino Uno (C++)
- **Sensors Used**:
  - Soil Moisture Sensor
  - Temperature Sensor (DHT11/22)
  - Light Sensor (LDR)
  - Water Level Sensor
- **Actuators**:
  - Relay Module
  - Water Pump
- **Software Tools**:
  - Arduino IDE
  - Fritzing (Circuit Diagram)
  - GitHub for collaboration 

---

## 🧪 How It Works
1. Sensors collect soil and environmental data continuously.  
2. AI analyzes the data to predict **optimal watering times and duration**.  
3. Water pump is automatically triggered to irrigate crops as needed.  
4. Real-time readings are stored in the app for tracking and analysis.  
5. AI adapts over time, improving predictions as more data is collected.
